## Known Limitations

### Current Version (v1.0.0)

1. **Fishing**
   - Automation logic based sa basic detection
   - May vary depending sa account reputation
   - Works best sa fresh/low-level accounts

2. **Quests**
   - Specific quest paths pre-programmed
   - May need manual adjustment para different scenarios

3. **Shop**
   - Prices ay fixed (manual update needed if prices change)
   - Limited item variants

4. **Webhooks**
   - Rate limiting: 1 notification per second minimum
   - Discord webhook ay kailangan monthly verification

5. **Performance**
   - Low graphics mode ay may limitations
   - FPS unlock may affect game stability sa some devices
